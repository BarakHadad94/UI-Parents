ABOUT EIN KEREM


Ein Kerem is an old and magical village lying in between Jerusalem's mountains.
It is one of the most visited places in Israel, known for its great green nature views and authentic old stone houses alongside sacred sites such as churches, monasteries and synagogues.

It is highly recommended to spend some time walking around the village between the narrow and colorful alleys, visit the artists places and enjoy the delightful local restaurants.
Ein Kerem is also a great starting spot for a trip all around Jerusalem for its well organized public transportation.

The Guest House
